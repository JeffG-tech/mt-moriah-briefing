Mt. Moriah Briefing

Proper Conduct & Property Title Updates

Bakit Tayo Nandito?

  • Faithfulness – “Consistent kahit walang nanonood.”
  • Integrity – “Walang kalokohan.”
  • Excellence – “Ayusin lahat, kahit maliit na bagay.”
  • Teamwork – “Mas mabilis pag sama-sama.”
  • Accountability – “Own your actions — wag sisihan.”

Ang Attitude, Mas Mabenta Kesa sa Property

Kapag nakilala ka ng client na maayos, honest, at professional — kahit hindi mo pa ibenta, bibili na sila sa’yo. Conduct creates credibility.

Ang Disiplina ng Mt. Moriah Agent

  • Bawal mangako ng sobra.
  • Bawal mang-‘agaw’ ng kliyente.
  • Bawal unauthorized site tripping.
  • Bawal disrespectful behavior — lalo na sa client or co-agent.

Hindi Ka lang Nagbebenta — Ikaw ang Representante ng May-ari

  • Tulungan ang clients sa documentation at follow-up.
  • Attend official site visits (recorded ha, para safe).
  • Gumamit lang ng approved templates at materials.
  • Never collect payments personally.

Authorized, Pero Alam ang Limitasyon

Do’s:

  • Sabihin palagi: “Authorized Marketing Associate under the property owner.”
  • Keep a copy ng affidavit for reference.
  • Direct lahat ng payments sa owner or office account.

Don’ts:

  • Wag magpanggap na realty o broker.
  • Wag gumawa ng legal assumptions or promises.

Legal Ka Ba? Let’s Make Sure!

Do’s:

  • Always record communications and receipts.
  • Gamitin lang ang official templates.
  • Mag-log ng clients para walang overlap.

Don’ts:

  • Wag tumanggap ng bayad sa personal GCash.
  • Wag magbigay ng timeline ng titulo kung hindi pa confirmed.
  • Wag gumawa ng sariling “promo.”

Benta Ka, Pero Di Ka Bawal!

FB DO’s:

  • Highlight location: “Malapit sa bypass road, tahimik, nature vibes.”
  • Add disclaimer: “Prices and availability may change. Message me for full details.”
  • Use 2–3 real photos lang.

FB DON’Ts:

  • Wag maglagay ng ₱rice/sqm, reservation fee, or downpayment details.
  • Wag ilagay exact address or map.
  • Wag ulit-ulitin ang parehong caption — spam ‘yan kay FB!
  • Wag mag-tag ng random people or groups.

Ang Profile Mo, Resume Mo!

  • Keep your name and profile consistent.
  • Be polite online, kahit mainit ulo ni client.
  • Avoid emotional rants, professionalism shows maturity.

Tahimik Pero Propesyonal

  • Wag i-post ang pangalan, resibo, or ID ng buyer.
  • Wag ilabas ang TCT numbers online.
  • Confidential dapat lahat ng transaction details.

Walang Banggaan, Lahat Kita!

  • Coordinate sa office bago magbigay ng updates.
  • I-report agad kung may fake or misleading posts.
  • Respect each other’s clients — teamwork, hindi competition.

Para Alam Mo Sagot sa Tanong na “Kumusta na Title?”

  • I-present ang current TCT progress (Moises & Napoleon Buyagawan).
  • Explain title stages: from mother title → subdivision → individual titles.
  • Emphasize transparency.

Benta Nang May Disiplina

  • Conduct creates credibility.
  • Credibility builds consistent income.
  • Always act under authority, never outside it.

Be Legal. Be Logical. Be Mt. Moriah.

Hindi kailangang lisensyado para maging disente. Ang kailangan lang — integrity, consistency, at respeto sa proseso. Yun ang Mt. Moriah standard.

Social Media Posting Rules

Para Di Ka Ma-Ban, Ma-Report, o Ma-Demanda!

Safe Zones Para sa mga Post

  • ✅ Facebook Marketplace
  • ✅ Buy & Sell Groups or Pages
  • ✅ Direct Messaging (Messenger, SMS, etc.)

Danger Zone — Avoid Posting These Publicly!

  • 🚫 Lot Plans or Subdivision Maps
  • 🚫 Price Lists & Installment Terms
  • 🚫 Reservation Fees, DP, ₱/sqm
  • 🚫 Exact Address or Google Map Pin
  • 🚫 Investment promises (‘sure profit’)

Hidden FB Red Flags!

  • Repetitive captions = Spam
  • Too many identical posts = Restriction
  • Posting financial details = Violation
  • Posting contracts or IDs = Privacy Violation
  • Tagging random people = Manipulation

Ito ang mga Ligtas I-Post

  • ✅ Location Highlights
  • ✅ Lifestyle Angles
  • ✅ Real Photos (2–3 Only)
  • ✅ Disclaimer

Mga Salitang May Sumpa!

  • 🚫 “Raw lots”
  • 🚫 “Installment / 3 years to pay”
  • 🚫 “Reservation Fee / DP”
  • 🚫 “₱ per sqm”
  • 🚫 “Guaranteed profit”

Pang-Professional Caption Lines

  • ✅ “Available Property Opportunities”
  • ✅ “Flexible payment options (PM for details)”
  • ✅ “Schedule a site visit for full details”

The Anatomy of a Good Post

  1. 1. Start with location or lifestyle.
  2. 2. Add soft call-to-action (CTA).
  3. 4. Use 2–3 authentic photos.

Sample Caption: Safe & Clean

“Helping families find peaceful property opportunities near Bayombong. Message me for details or to schedule a visit.”

Tips Para Hindi Ma-ban

  • ✅ Limit to 5 varied group posts per day.
  • ✅ Always use different photo sets.
  • ✅ Reply to comments with DM, not numbers.
  • ✅ Review group rules before posting.
  • ✅ Delete old duplicate posts.

Your Online Business Card

  • ✅ Post professionally, not personally.
  • ✅ Respond politely, even to rudeness.
  • ✅ Maintain a consistent, trustworthy profile.
  • ✅ Every post reflects your credibility.
