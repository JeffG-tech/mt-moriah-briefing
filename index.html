Conduct Creates Credibility.

Ang Disiplina ng Mt. Moriah Agent

Ang pagiging agent ay hindi basta-basta. Dapat transparent, respectful, at compliant.

Isa lang ang bawal dito: ang pasaway.

  • Bawal mangako ng sobra.
  • Bawal mang-‘agaw’ ng kliyente.
  • Bawal unauthorized site tripping.
  • Bawal disrespectful behavior.

Ikaw ang Representante ng May-ari

Tayo ay Marketing Associates authorized by Mr. Moises, the property owner. Hindi tayo broker, hindi tayo realty — direct tayo buyer to seller.

  • Tulungan ang clients sa documentation at follow-up.
  • Attend official site visits (recorded for safety).
  • Gumamit lang ng approved templates at materials.
  • Never collect payments personally.

Social Media Posting Rules

Para Di Ka Ma-Ban, Ma-Report, o Ma-Demanda!

Social media is your marketplace — pero dapat marunong kang maglaro sa rules.

Ang goal: maging visible sa Facebook nang di lumalabag sa rules ni FB o sa batas ng Pilipinas.

FB & Marketplace Safety

"Alam mo bang minsan, hindi tao ang nagre-report sa’yo — si Facebook mismo! Kaya dapat marunong tayong umiwas sa red flags."

🚫 MGA BAWAL I-POST PUBLICLY 🚫

  • ₱/sqm, reservation fee, or downpayment details.
  • Lot Plans or Exact Address.
  • Investment promises ('sure profit').
  • Copied captions or images.

Good Post vs. Bad Post

❌ BAD POST (High Risk)

"Murang Lupa for Sale in Bayombong!
Only ₱2,500/sqm! Reserve now for ₱10,000!
3 years to pay, installment basis.
Address: Purok 5, Brgy. Masipag
Guaranteed profit in 2 years! PM is the key!"

✅ GOOD POST (Safe & Professional)

"Helping families find peaceful property opportunities near Bayombong.
Ideal for your future home. Enjoy the fresh air and nature vibes!
Message me for full details or to schedule a site visit.

Disclaimer: I am a Marketing Associate authorized by the property owner."

Client Confidentiality & Data Privacy

Hindi lahat ng 'proof' kailangang i-post. Mas ok na tahimik pero maayos, kesa maingay pero delikado.

STRICTLY NO POSTING OF:

  • Pangalan, resibo, or ID ng buyer.
  • TCT numbers online.
  • Confidential transaction details.
